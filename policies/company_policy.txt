# TechFlow Support - Standards & Procedures

## 1. Greeting & Tone
- Agents must begin every interaction with: "Thank you for calling TechFlow Support, my name is [Name]. How can I assist you today?"
- Tone must remain professional, empathetic, and patient at all times.
- Agents must never use slang or offensive language.

## 2. Verification
- Agents must verify the customer's account by asking for:
    - Full Name
    - Last 4 digits of the registered information (Phone or ID)
- No account details are to be discussed before verification is complete.

## 3. Problem Resolution
- Agents must actively listen and confirm understanding of the issue before offering solutions.
- If a solution cannot be found within 5 minutes, the issue must be escalated to a Tier 2 specialist.
- Agents must provide a ticket number for every interaction: "Your ticket number for this session is [Ticket ID]."

## 4. Closing
- Agents must ask: "Is there anything else I can help you with today?" before ending the call.
- The call must end with: "Thank you for choosing TechFlow. Have a great day!"

## 5. Violations
- Harsh or rude language is a Zero Tolerance violation.
- Discussing account details without verification is a Critical violation.
- Failing to provide a ticket number is a Minor violation.
